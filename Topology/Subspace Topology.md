#definition
### Types
- $\left( X, \tau \right)$ : [[Topological Space]]
- `S` : Subset of `X`
### Definition
We define the *subspace [[topology]]* on `S` as:
$$
\begin{aligned}
\tau_{S} = \left\{ U\subset S, \exists V \in \tau, S\cap U \in \tau \right\} 
\end{aligned}
$$
##### aka
- Relative topology
