### Types
- `M` : [[Topological Space]]
- $\{U_{\alpha}\}_{\alpha\in\text{A}}$ : [[Covering]] of `M`
- $\{V_{\alpha}\}_{\alpha\in\text{A}}$ : [[Covering]] of `M`
### Definition
$\{V_{\beta}\}_{\beta\in\text{B}}$ is a *refinement* of $\{U_{\alpha}\}_{\alpha\in\text{A}}$ if each $V_{\beta}$  is contained in some $U_{\alpha}$.
