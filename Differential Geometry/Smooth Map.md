#definition
### Types
- `n` `m` : $\mathbb{N}$
- `N` : [[Smooth Manifold]] of Dimension `n` 
- `M` : [[Smooth Manifold]] of Dimension `m` 
- $\phi : M \to N$
### Definition
$\phi$ is a *smooth map* if for every [[chart]] $\left( U, \varphi \right)$ on `M` and $\left( V,\psi \right)$ on `N`, the map $\psi \circ \phi \circ \varphi^{-1}$ is smooth.
