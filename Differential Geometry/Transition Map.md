#definition
### Types 
- $\left(U, \varphi\right)$ $\left(V, \psi\right)$ : [[Chart]] 
### Definition
If $U \cap V \ne 0$, the map  $\psi \circ \varphi^{-1} : \varphi\left(U\cap V\right) \to\psi\left(U\cap V\right)$ is called a *transition map*.
##### aka
- Transition Function
- Change of coordinates
